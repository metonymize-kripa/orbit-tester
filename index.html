The free-energy FUQ (Frequently Unasked Questions)

A site where free-energy and related concepts are explored using the mnemonic medium developed by Orbit.

The Questions

  • Can you give me an example for free-energy?
  • Is there a toy math model I can build to understand it?
  • In what way does free-energy relate to the Free Energy in thermodynamics?
  • Can it apply to non-living things?
  • Why is it the free-energy //principle// Vs theory or hypothesis?
  • Why did Karl Friston need the FEP?
  • What is the next best "unifying brain theory" if we drop FEP?
  • Are there cool applications of FEP?
  • What are the changes that could make it better?
